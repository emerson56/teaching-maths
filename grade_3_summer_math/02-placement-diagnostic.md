# Placement Diagnostic

Give this before Week 1. Do not teach during the diagnostic. Watch how the student thinks.

## Number Sense

1. Read and write: `4,706`, `3,090`, `8,215`.
2. Write `6,384` in expanded form.
3. Compare: `4,099 __ 4,900`.
4. Round `647` to the nearest 10 and nearest 100.

## Addition and Subtraction

1. `468 + 275`
2. `902 - 476`
3. Mentally: `399 + 58`
4. Mentally: `1000 - 250`
5. Mentally: `75 + 68`

## Multiplication Readiness

1. Draw 4 groups of 6.
2. Write an equation for 3 rows of 7.
3. Skip-count by 3, 4, 6, and 8.

## Division Readiness

1. Share 24 counters among 6 people.
2. How many groups of 5 can be made from 35?

## Fractions

1. Shade `1/3` of a rectangle.
2. Which is larger, `1/4` or `1/8`? Explain.

## Word Problem

Lena has 4 bags with 6 marbles in each bag. She gives 5 marbles away. How many marbles does she have now?

## Scoring

- 80%+ with clear explanations: use the program as written and add challenge problems.
- 50-79%: use the program as written.
- Below 50%: spend one extra week on place value, addition/subtraction within 1000, arrays, and equal groups before Week 2.
