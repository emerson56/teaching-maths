# Florida Alignment

Florida B.E.S.T. is the program floor, not the ceiling.

## Grade 3 B.E.S.T. Coverage

This program covers:

- Whole numbers through 10,000.
- Place value, composing, decomposing, comparing, and rounding.
- Multi-digit addition and subtraction.
- Multiplication and related division facts through 12.
- One- and two-step real-world problems using all four operations.
- Fractions as numbers, including fractions greater than one.
- Equivalent fractions, comparing fractions, and number-line models.
- Length, liquid volume, temperature, time, and elapsed time.
- Points, lines, line segments, rays, intersecting lines, parallel lines, and perpendicular lines.
- Quadrilaterals and defining attributes.
- Area and perimeter.
- Scaled pictographs, scaled bar graphs, line plots, and simple circle graphs.

## FAST Preparation

FAST format familiarity appears every other Friday through a few multiple-choice or computer-style items. It should never dominate instruction.

The goal is:

- No surprise from test format.
- Strong reasoning under ordinary conditions.
- Calm checking habits.

## Mathematical Thinking and Reasoning

Every lesson should include at least one of these habits:

- Persevere through a non-routine task.
- Represent the same problem in more than one way.
- Explain and justify a solution.
- Compare strategies for efficiency.
- Check whether an answer is reasonable.
- Connect new ideas to previous math.
- Apply math to a real or authentic context.
