# Day 5: Multiplication and Division Assessment

## Goal

Demonstrate strategic fluency through 12 and explain multiplication-division relationships.

## Materials

Weekly assessment, counters, grid paper.

## Number Talk

Which fact strategy is most useful for `7 x 8`?

## Main Lesson

Review the strategy menu:

- Known fact.
- Turn-around fact.
- Double.
- Double a double.
- 5 plus more groups.
- 10 minus or plus groups.
- Unknown factor.

## Practice

- Complete the Week 4 assessment.
- Add four FAST-style questions.
- Correct missed facts by deriving them, not copying them.
- Create a personal "facts still learning" list.

## Challenge

Use exactly four multiplication or division equations to connect 6, 7, 8, 42, 48, and 56.

## Exit Check

Explain the difference between knowing a fact and being able to derive it efficiently.
