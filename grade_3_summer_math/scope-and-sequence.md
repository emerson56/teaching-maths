# Scope and Sequence

## Week 1: Place Value to 10,000 and Mental Math

Read, write, compare, order, and round numbers to 10,000. Use mental addition and subtraction strategies.

## Week 2: Addition, Subtraction, and Two-Step Word Problems

Add and subtract within 1,000. Use bar models, equations, estimation, and inverse operations.

## Week 3: Multiplication Foundations

Understand equal groups, arrays, skip-counting, commutativity, and facts for 2, 3, 4, 5, and 10.

## Week 4: Multiplication and Division 6-12

Build fluency through 12 x 12. Relate multiplication and division. Interpret remainders.

## Week 5: Properties, Variables, and Multi-Step Problems

Use the commutative, associative, and distributive properties. Solve missing-number equations and two-step problems.

## Week 6: Fractions

Understand fractions as numbers, use number lines, compare fractions, find equivalent fractions, and handle fractions greater than one.

## Week 7: Measurement and Data

Measure length, volume, mass, time, money, and temperature. Create and interpret graphs.

## Week 8: Geometry, Area, and Perimeter

Study points, lines, rays, quadrilaterals, area, perimeter, and the relationship between area and multiplication.

## Week 9: Integrated Problem Solving and Enrichment

Combine arithmetic, measurement, fractions, data, and geometry through non-routine problems.

## Week 10: Review, Assessment, and Grade 4 Preview

Review Grade 3, assess mastery, correct gaps, complete a performance task, and preview Grade 4 ideas.
