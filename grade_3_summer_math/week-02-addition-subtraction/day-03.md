# Day 3: Bar Models and Part-Whole Reasoning

## Goal

Represent unknown parts, wholes, and comparisons with bar models and equations.

## Materials

Paper strips, ruler, colored pencils.

## Number Talk

The whole is 850 and one part is 327. What is the other part?

## Main Lesson

1. Draw a bar model for part + part = whole.
2. Draw a comparison model for "358 more than 467."
3. Label known and unknown quantities.
4. Write an equation with a box or letter for the unknown.
5. Solve and check the answer in the story.

## Practice

- Find an unknown part.
- Find an unknown whole.
- Solve a "more than" comparison.
- Solve a "less than" comparison.
- Match four equations to four bar models.

## Challenge

A number is 358 more than 467 and 129 less than another number. Find the other number.

## Exit Check

Draw a bar model for `x + 275 = 902`.
