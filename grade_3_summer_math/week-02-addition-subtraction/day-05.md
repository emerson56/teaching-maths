# Day 5: Mixed Assessment and Strategy Choice

## Goal

Choose efficient addition/subtraction strategies and explain why they work.

## Materials

Weekly assessment, base-ten blocks available but not required.

## Number Talk

Which strategy is best for `702 - 398`: regrouping, compensation, or counting up?

## Main Lesson

Sort six problems into:

- Mental strategy.
- Written algorithm.
- Estimate only.

Explain each choice before solving.

## Practice

- Complete the Week 2 assessment.
- Include four brief FAST-style questions.
- Correct mistakes and name the cause: reading, computation, model, or checking.

## Challenge

Use digits 0-9 at most once to create an addition equation with a sum closest to 1,000.

## Exit Check

Solve `902 - 476` and explain the regrouping in words.
