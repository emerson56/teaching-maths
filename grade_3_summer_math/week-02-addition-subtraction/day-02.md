# Day 2: Subtraction With Regrouping and Zeros

## Goal

Subtract within 1,000 and explain regrouping across zeros.

## Materials

Base-ten blocks, place-value chart, grid paper.

## Number Talk

Solve `500 - 198` without a standard algorithm.

## Main Lesson

1. Model `902 - 476`.
2. Regroup 1 hundred as 10 tens, then 1 ten as 10 ones.
3. Connect every trade to the written algorithm.
4. Check using addition.
5. Compare with counting up from 476 to 902.

## Practice

- `731 - 286`
- `600 - 347`
- `1,000 - 458`
- `804 - 179`
- Check two answers with addition.

## Challenge

Find two different subtraction equations with a difference of 426 and explain how you created them.

## Exit Check

Why can we not simply do `2 - 6` in `902 - 476`?
