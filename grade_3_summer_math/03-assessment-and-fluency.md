# Assessment and Fluency

## Friday Assessment Routine

Each Friday:

- 10 fluency questions.
- 4 computation questions.
- 3 word problems.
- 1 explanation problem.
- 1 challenge problem.

Every other week:

- Add 4-6 Florida FAST-style multiple-choice or computer-style items.

## Mastery Labels

Secure:

- Accurate.
- Explains clearly.
- Can solve a variation.

Developing:

- Handles routine cases.
- Needs prompting, drawings, or reminders.

Fragile:

- May get the answer, but reasoning is unclear.
- Procedure breaks when the problem changes.

## Fact Fluency Sequence

Teach facts by leverage, not just numeric order.

1. 2s, 5s, 10s.
2. 3s and 4s.
3. Squares: `3 x 3`, `4 x 4`, `5 x 5`.
4. 6s using 5s plus one group.
5. 8s using doubling.
6. 9s using 10s minus one group.
7. 7s using known facts.
8. 11s and 12s using place value, distributive reasoning, and known facts.

## Daily Fluency

Use 8-10 minutes:

- 2 minutes: strategy talk.
- 3 minutes: flash or oral practice.
- 3 minutes: related division facts.
- 2 minutes: written mixed review.

Timed work is allowed only after strategies are secure.
