# Materials

## Core Spine

- Singapore Primary Mathematics 3A/3B or Dimensions Math 3A/3B.

## Enrichment Spine

- Beast Academy 3A-3D.

Use especially for:

- Puzzles.
- Geometry.
- Variables.
- Deep word problems.
- Mathematical play.

## Manipulatives

- Base-ten blocks or place-value disks.
- Square tiles or graph paper.
- Fraction strips or fraction circles.
- Ruler marked in inches, half-inches, and quarter-inches.
- Measuring cups.
- Kitchen scale.
- Analog clock.
- Play money.
- Multiplication chart as a reasoning tool, not a crutch.

## Optional Digital Practice

- Khan Academy 3rd grade.
- Beast Academy Online.
- Florida FAST sample items for occasional format familiarity.
