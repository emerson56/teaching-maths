# Day 4: Corrections and Targeted Reteaching

## Goal

Turn final-assessment errors into durable learning.

## Materials

Final assessment, manipulatives matched to errors, error log.

## Number Talk

What is the difference between a careless error and a misunderstanding?

## Main Lesson

For each important error:

1. Identify what the student was thinking.
2. Classify the issue: reading, concept, computation, representation, or checking.
3. Rebuild the idea with a concrete or visual model.
4. Solve a simpler related case.
5. Retry the original problem.
6. Solve one transfer problem.

## Practice

Focus on no more than three priority gaps.

## Challenge

Ask the student to design a "trap answer" and explain why someone might choose it.

## Exit Check

Complete: "I used to think ____. Now I understand ____."
