# Day 2: Written Final Assessment

## Goal

Measure independent mastery across the Grade 3 program.

## Materials

Final assessment, ruler, scratch paper. Manipulatives available only if the item permits them.

## Assessment Sections

1. Place value and rounding.
2. Addition and subtraction.
3. Multiplication and division through 12.
4. Variables and two-step problems.
5. Fractions.
6. Measurement and data.
7. Geometry, area, and perimeter.
8. One non-routine challenge.

## Administration

- Allow 60-75 minutes with one short break.
- Do not coach.
- Ask the student to mark any uncertain answer.
- Record strategy observations.

## Reflection

After finishing, ask:

- Which item required the most thinking?
- Which item felt satisfying?
- Where did you check your work?

## Exit Check

No corrections today. End with an enjoyable math game.
