# Day 3: Oral Problem-Solving Interview

## Goal

Assess reasoning, flexibility, mathematical language, and response to unfamiliar problems.

## Materials

Counters, grid paper, fraction strips, interview notes.

## Interview Problems

1. Solve `8 x 7` in two ways.
2. Explain `902 - 476`.
3. Compare `2/3` and `2/5`.
4. Draw two rectangles with area 24.
5. Solve a two-step story.
6. Attempt one unfamiliar logic or counting problem.

## Interview Prompts

- Why does that work?
- Can you show another representation?
- How could you check?
- What would happen if the numbers changed?

## Scoring

Record:

- Accuracy.
- Strategy flexibility.
- Precision of language.
- Independence.
- Persistence.

## Exit Check

Tell the student one specific mathematical habit that has grown.
