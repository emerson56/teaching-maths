# Day 1: Cumulative Review Game

## Goal

Retrieve and connect major Grade 3 ideas without relying on blocked practice.

## Materials

Question cards, dice, whiteboard, score tokens if desired.

## Number Talk

Name three different topics that connect to multiplication.

## Main Lesson

Create stations or card groups:

- Place value and computation.
- Multiplication and division.
- Fractions.
- Measurement and data.
- Geometry.
- Challenge problems.

Require an explanation or model for full credit.

## Practice

- Complete at least three questions from every domain.
- Mark uncertain topics for Day 4 reteaching.
- Add one favorite question to the beautiful-problems notebook.

## Challenge

Create one problem that combines three Grade 3 domains.

## Exit Check

List the two topics that feel strongest and the two that need review.
