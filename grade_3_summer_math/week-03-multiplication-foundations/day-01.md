# Day 1: Equal Groups and Repeated Addition

## Goal

Interpret multiplication as equal groups and connect it to repeated addition.

## Materials

Counters, cups or paper circles, whiteboard.

## Number Talk

What is the difference between 4 groups of 6 and 6 groups of 4?

## Main Lesson

1. Make 4 equal groups of 6 counters.
2. Record `6 + 6 + 6 + 6 = 24`.
3. Record `4 x 6 = 24`.
4. Identify number of groups, size of each group, and total.
5. Contrast with unequal groups.

## Practice

- Model `3 x 7`, `5 x 4`, and `2 x 9`.
- Match group drawings to equations.
- Draw a model for `6 x 3`.
- Write a story for `4 x 8`.

## Challenge

There are 24 counters. Find every way to arrange them into equal groups.

## Exit Check

Explain what each number means in `5 x 7 = 35`.
