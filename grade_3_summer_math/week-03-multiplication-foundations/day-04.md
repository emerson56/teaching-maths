# Day 4: Facts for 2, 5, and 10 With Division

## Goal

Develop fluent strategies for 2s, 5s, and 10s and connect related division facts.

## Materials

Counters, fact-family triangles, clock face, play money.

## Number Talk

If `5 x 8 = 40`, what other facts can you know immediately?

## Main Lesson

1. Use doubles for 2s.
2. Use clock and money patterns for 5s.
3. Use place-value shifts for 10s.
4. Build fact families such as `5 x 8 = 40`, `8 x 5 = 40`, `40 / 5 = 8`, `40 / 8 = 5`.

## Practice

- Mixed 2, 5, and 10 multiplication.
- Related division facts.
- Missing-factor equations.
- Two short equal-group stories.

## Challenge

Without counting one by one, find how many hands are on 18 people and explain the strategy.

## Exit Check

Write the complete fact family for 7, 10, and 70.
