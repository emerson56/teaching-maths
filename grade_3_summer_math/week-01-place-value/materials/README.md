# Week 1 Day 1 Materials

## Printable Files

- `week-01-day-01-student-packet.docx`: student worksheets and reusable manipulatives.
- `week-01-day-01-student-packet.pdf`: print-ready student packet.
- `week-01-day-01-teacher-guide.docx`: scripted teaching manual with theory, material use, exact teacher language, misconceptions, answers, and observation guidance.
- `week-01-day-01-teacher-guide.pdf`: print-ready scripted teaching manual.

## Student Packet Contents

1. Quick number-sense diagnostic.
2. Mental-math diagnostic.
3. Four-column place-value mat.
4. Build and record practice.
5. Number forms practice.
6. Decomposition and trading practice.
7. Fourteen-piece challenge.
8. Exit ticket.
9. Cut-apart digit cards.
10. Cut-apart thousands and hundreds.
11. Cut-apart tens and ones.

## Printing

- Print pages 1-8 normally for the student.
- Print pages 9-11 on cardstock if the manipulatives will be reused.
- The place-value mat can be placed in a sheet protector and used with a dry-erase marker.
