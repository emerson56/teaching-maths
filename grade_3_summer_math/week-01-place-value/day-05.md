# Day 5: Estimation and Place-Value Problems

## Goal

Use place value and rounding to solve problems and judge reasonableness.

## Materials

Weekly assessment paper, number line, digit cards.

## Number Talk

Is `2,984 + 3,121` closer to 5,000, 6,000, or 7,000? Explain.

## Main Lesson

Solve together:

A library has 2,487 fiction books and 1,536 nonfiction books. About how many books are there? Then find the exact total.

Discuss when an estimate is enough and when an exact answer is needed.

## Practice

- Estimate, solve, and check three addition/subtraction stories.
- Complete the Week 1 assessment.
- Correct every missed item in a different color.

## Challenge

I am a four-digit number. My hundreds digit is twice my thousands digit. My ones digit is 3 less than my tens digit. Find all possible answers.

## Exit Check

Write one thing that is secure and one skill to revisit next week.
