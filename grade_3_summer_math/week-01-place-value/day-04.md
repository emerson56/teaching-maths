# Day 4: Mental Addition and Subtraction

## Goal

Use compensation, making friendly numbers, and place-value decomposition.

## Materials

Mini whiteboard, number line, place-value chart.

## Number Talk

Solve `399 + 58` in at least two ways.

## Main Lesson

1. Solve `398 + 47` by adding 2 to make 400, then adjusting.
2. Solve `1,000 - 248` by subtracting 250 and adding 2.
3. Decompose `75 + 68` into tens and ones.
4. Compare the strategies and decide which is most efficient for each problem.

## Practice

Solve mentally and explain:

- `497 + 36`
- `603 - 198`
- `250 + 475`
- `900 - 375`
- `1,250 + 99`

## Challenge

Without writing a standard algorithm, find `1,998 + 2,047` and justify every adjustment.

## Exit Check

Complete: "Compensation is useful when..."
