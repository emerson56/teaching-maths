# Day 2: Expanded Form and Comparing Numbers

## Goal

Write four-digit numbers in multiple forms and compare them using place value.

## Materials

Place-value chart, digit cards, index cards with `<`, `>`, and `=`.

## Number Talk

Which is closer to 5,000: `4,920` or `5,081`? Explain without subtracting exactly.

## Main Lesson

1. Write `6,384` as `6,000 + 300 + 80 + 4`.
2. Write it in words and unit form.
3. Compare `6,305` and `6,053` from the greatest place first.
4. Show why checking the ones first is unreliable.
5. Order four student-created numbers.

## Practice

- Write `9,042`, `3,590`, and `7,007` in standard, word, and expanded form.
- Compare `4,099` and `4,900`.
- Order `2,730`, `2,703`, `2,370`, `2,307`.
- Insert a digit to make `5,_42 > 5,642`.

## Challenge

Use digits 1, 3, 5, and 8 exactly once to make the greatest and least possible numbers. Find the difference.

## Exit Check

Explain why `6,305 > 6,053` using place-value language.
