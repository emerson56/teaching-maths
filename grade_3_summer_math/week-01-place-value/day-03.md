# Day 3: Number Lines and Rounding

## Goal

Locate four-digit numbers and round to the nearest 10, 100, and 1,000.

## Materials

Open number lines, sticky notes, ruler or tape measure.

## Number Talk

What two multiples of 100 surround `647`? Which is closer?

## Main Lesson

1. Place `647` between 600 and 700 on an open number line.
2. Mark the midpoint, 650.
3. Use distance, not a memorized rhyme, to round.
4. Repeat for nearest 10 and nearest 1,000.
5. Discuss halfway cases and why they round to the greater benchmark.

## Practice

Round:

- `372` to nearest 10 and 100.
- `1,649` to nearest 10, 100, and 1,000.
- `8,501` to nearest 100 and 1,000.
- Find all whole numbers that round to 430 to the nearest 10.

## Challenge

A number rounds to 3,000 to the nearest thousand and 3,400 to the nearest hundred. What could it be?

## Exit Check

Draw a number line proving that `2,649` rounds to `2,600` to the nearest hundred.
