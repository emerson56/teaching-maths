# Day 4: Money and Making Change

## Goal

Add and subtract money amounts and solve making-change problems.

## Materials

Play money, store item cards, receipt paper.

## Number Talk

You buy something for $3.75 and pay with $5.00. How can you count up to find the change?

## Main Lesson

1. Review dollars and cents notation.
2. Add item prices.
3. Subtract to find change.
4. Count up as an alternate strategy.
5. Discuss estimating to catch unreasonable totals.

## Practice

- Make a small store with prices.
- Buy two or three items and find total cost.
- Pay with $10 or $20 and find change.
- Write two word problems from the store.

## Challenge

A customer buys 4 items and receives $6.35 change from $20. Create possible item prices.

## Exit Check

Explain why $4.07 is not the same as $4.70.
