# Day 3: Area of Rectangles

## Goal

Find the area of rectangles using multiplication.

## Materials

Grid paper, square tiles, ruler.

## Number Talk

How does a `4 x 7` array help find the area of a rectangle?

## Main Lesson

1. Tile a 4-by-7 rectangle.
2. Count by rows and columns.
3. Record area as length times width.
4. Connect rectangle area to arrays.
5. Use decomposition for larger rectangles.

## Practice

- Find areas of several rectangles.
- Draw rectangles for given dimensions.
- Find missing side length when area and one side are known.
- Solve one area word problem.

## Challenge

Draw all rectangles with whole-number side lengths and area 24. Compare their shapes.

## Exit Check

Explain why a rectangle 3 units by 8 units has area 24 square units.
