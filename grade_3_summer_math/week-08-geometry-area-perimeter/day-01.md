# Day 1: Points, Lines, Rays, and Polygons

## Goal

Describe and draw points, lines, line segments, rays, intersecting lines, parallel lines, perpendicular lines, and polygons.

## Materials

Ruler, grid paper, geometry cards.

## Number Talk

What is the same and different about a line segment and a ray?

## Main Lesson

1. Draw and label a point.
2. Draw and label a line, segment, and ray.
3. Identify intersecting, parallel, and perpendicular lines.
4. Build polygons from line segments.
5. Sort shapes and non-shapes by attributes.

## Practice

- Draw each geometry object.
- Identify examples around the room.
- Classify triangles, quadrilaterals, pentagons, and hexagons.
- Explain why a curved shape is not a polygon.

## Challenge

Draw a shape with exactly one pair of parallel sides and at least one right angle.

## Exit Check

Explain the difference between parallel and perpendicular lines.
