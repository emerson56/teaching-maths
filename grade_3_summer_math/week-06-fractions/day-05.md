# Day 5: Fractions Greater Than One

## Goal

Read, write, model, and locate fractions greater than one.

## Materials

Fraction strips, number lines extending to 3, weekly assessment.

## Number Talk

What does `5/4` mean?

## Main Lesson

1. Build `5/4` as five one-fourth pieces.
2. Locate `5/4` between 1 and 2.
3. Read it as five fourths.
4. Connect `4/4`, `8/4`, and `12/4` to whole numbers.
5. Introduce mixed-number language as optional enrichment.

## Practice

- Model and locate `7/3`, `6/4`, and `9/8`.
- Identify whole-number fractions.
- Complete the Week 6 assessment with FAST-style items.
- Correct mistakes using models.

## Challenge

Find all fractions with denominator 4 that lie between 1 and 3.

## Exit Check

Draw a number line showing why `6/4 > 1`.
