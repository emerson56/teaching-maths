# Day 4: Comparing Fractions

## Goal

Compare fractions with common numerators or denominators and justify comparisons.

## Materials

Fraction strips, number lines, comparison cards.

## Number Talk

Which is larger: `3/8` or `5/8`? What stays the same?

## Main Lesson

1. Compare equal denominators by counting equal-size parts.
2. Compare equal numerators by reasoning about part size.
3. Use benchmarks 0, `1/2`, and 1.
4. Require the same whole.
5. Write comparisons using `<`, `>`, and `=`.

## Practice

- Compare `3/7` and `5/7`.
- Compare `2/3` and `2/5`.
- Compare `4/8` and `1/2`.
- Explain one comparison with a number line.

## Challenge

Which is larger: `3/4` of a small pizza or `1/2` of a large pizza? Explain what information is missing.

## Exit Check

Explain why a larger denominator can mean smaller pieces.
