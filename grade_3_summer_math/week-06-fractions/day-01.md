# Day 1: Equal Parts and Unit Fractions

## Goal

Understand a unit fraction as one equal part of a whole.

## Materials

Paper strips, fraction circles, scissors, crayons.

## Number Talk

Which is larger, one half or one fourth? Why?

## Main Lesson

1. Fold equal-sized strips into halves, thirds, fourths, sixths, and eighths.
2. Name one part as `1/b`.
3. Explain that the denominator tells how many equal parts make the whole.
4. Contrast equal and unequal partitions.
5. Keep the whole fixed when comparing.

## Practice

- Partition shapes into equal parts.
- Label unit fractions.
- Identify non-examples.
- Order several unit fractions using strips.

## Challenge

Can a shape be divided into four equal areas that do not have the same shape? Create an example.

## Exit Check

Explain why `1/8 < 1/4` for the same whole.
